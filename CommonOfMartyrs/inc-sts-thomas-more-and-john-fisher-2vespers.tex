\newcommand{\collecttranslation}{O God, who didst raise up from among the English people Thy blessed Martyrs, John and Thomas, to be zealous defenders of the true Faith and of the primacy of the Roman Church~: grant through their merits and prayers that, by the practice of the same Faith, we may all become and remain united in Christ.}
