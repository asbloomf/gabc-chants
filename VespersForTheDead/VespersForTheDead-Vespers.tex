	\Vbar{}~Et ne nos indúcas in tentatiónem.
	
	\Rbar{}~Sed líbera nos a malo.

	\Vbar{}~A porta ínferi.  \Rbar{}~Erue Dómine ánimas eórum [ánimam ejus].

	\Vbar{}~Requiéscant in páce [Requiéscat in pace].  \Rbar{}~Amen.

	\Vbar{}~Dómine exáudi oratiónem meam.  \Rbar{}~Et clamor meus ad te véniat.

	\Vbar{}~Dóminus vobíscum.  \Rbar{}~Et cum spíritu tuo.

	\medskip

	{\centering


	Orémus.


	\smallskip

	For deceased Brethren, Relations, and Benefactors.

	\smallskip

	\emph{Prayer.}

	}
	\smallskip


}
